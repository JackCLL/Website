HOME ABOUT PLANNING THE SOUL SANTION

The idea of the soul sanction is that of a time capsule, keeping the memory of a persons fortune. You could considered as treasure and paradise for ones who find it. Its a space for people to interact and enjoy events together. Its a garden, a garage, a theater, a pool and a library in one space. The soul sanction would be design as a timeless space where outside interference would not damage the space.
